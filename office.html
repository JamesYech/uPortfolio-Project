In March 2017, a sewer line cracked and flooded part of the basement. Of course, this was the location of my office. I had to rip out all the drywall, studs, and carpeting. It wasn't totally unplanned as I wanted to remodel anyway, just a bad time of year. It took a few months but now I have a nice wrap around desk on three walls and a french-cleat shelving system on two.
