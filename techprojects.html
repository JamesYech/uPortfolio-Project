JAMES YECH

Network Administrator

While IT Director for a public school district, I managed the design and installation of a new network infrastructure. All existing wiring was replaced, new drops added, a 100% coverage wifi network installed, and all new switches installed.

Shortly after the infrastructure rebuild, I facilitated a 1-to-1 device rollout for grades 2-12. This was done in stages over three years so devices and classroom processes could be evaluated ensuring a successful implementation.
